CEDAR

Characterizing Electron Densities at Reionization

CEDAR is a Cycle 5 JWST program (ID 10518; PI: N. Cleri) targeting the EGS with four pointings each of G140H and G395H NIRSpec MSA, designed to target [O II] and C III] in z>6 galaxies.
