I am an extragalactic astronomer who uses spectroscopy to study galaxies across cosmic time. My work revolves around use of large surveys from primarily space-based observatories like the Hubble Space Telescope (HST) and the James Webb Space Telescope (JWST). I am currently working as a part of the NGDEEP and CEERS collaborations. Here you will find some selected publications and results from my recent work. See all of my publications on ADS.
